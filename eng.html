We are visited by men of different age and position. It’s their
attitude to life and moral principles that put them together. They live for what
they do. They are completely dedicated to their profession.

FIRM Barbershop – it is men’s hair salon in Kiev, Moscow,
Minsk, Yerevan, Zaporozhye and Vilnius.

FIRM Barbershop Moscow – today there are 3 hairdressers, which are
located in different parts of Moscow (metro station Arbatskaya,
Kurskaya, and Krasnye Vorota). Each one has his own charm and
specifics, but at the same time – the overall style remains the same
as the quality of the haircuts.

Our team is presented by qualified men’s hairdressers with experience
from 7 years which, from year to year improve their skills. The main
cosmetics and brands used in the work of the masters, you can find
only in FIRM.

A FIRM IS NOT JUST A HAIRCUT AND NOT A STYLE. And not even the quality. We think of more pronounced categories. Just haircuts - in any hairdressing salon, and in FIRM - atmosphere, soul and meaning.

QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

We cut by hand and with a clipper, shave and shape the beard with a clipper and straight razor, wash and style the hair. We sell men's cosmetics, pour coffee or whiskey and offer SIGNATURE merch.

Shampoos and conditioners, hair and beard styling and care products, perfumes, toothpastes, combs, blades and razors. No mass market - only professional products from proven brands of men's cosmetics. The assortment can be found on the store page.

The history of the FIRM brand dates back to 2012, when the first branch was opened in Kyiv. Moscow life of FIRM began in 2014 with the opening of the first Moscow branch in Merzlyakovsky Lane near the Arbat. For six years, 10,000 people visited the walls of the branch, one ton of coffee was irrevocably and happily spent, and our masters cut 25,000 beards. Two other Moscow branches opened in 2015 and 2016.
